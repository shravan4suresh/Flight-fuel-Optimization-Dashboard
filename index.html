Overview

This dashboard treats taxi-out delay as an operational optimization problem. The workflow starts with flight movement records, enriches them with weather and congestion indicators, predicts taxi-out minutes, and then estimates the downstream fuel, cost, and emissions effect.

Prediction

Forecast expected taxi-out duration before departure using airport, runway, weather, aircraft, and congestion signals.

Optimization

Estimate avoidable minutes from better pushback sequencing, gate holds, or congestion-aware departure planning.

Impact

Convert delay minutes into extra fuel, fuel cost, and CO2 emissions using aircraft-specific burn rates.

Wind Components

Transform raw wind speed into headwind, tailwind, and crosswind components relative to each assigned runway.

CO2 Impact

Jet fuel emissions are approximated using 3.16 kilograms of CO2 per kilogram of fuel. The optimization case shows potential emissions avoided when taxi delays are reduced.
